About us:

Hello! We are Leo Deng, Tim Gu, Zheng Hanchen, Kevin Tan, Ethan Dai,
Johnny Zhang, Jack Huang, William Wang,
Liya Ma, Mr. Rumley, and Mr. Mac from Keystone academy.
Mars Club's mission is to evacuate Earth and go to Mars in order to avoid
the problems from Earth. Mars Club was founded in 2018 by Leo Deng,
Tim Gu, Liya Ma, and Roi Dai. We thought about this idea to evacuate
Earth because we read a lot of articles about disasters on where we live.
We started with four people, and then Mr. Rumley, Ethan Dai,
William Wang, Ma Zheng from Yale, and Alan Guo from Stanford
joined our club. In 2020, we talked to Mr. McKenzie, our head of school,
about our idea. We decided to make a KAP to spread our idea.
We planned to complete our mission in the future with 3 main steps;
Spread, Prepare, and Action. Spread is the step when we spread our ideas
to other people. Prepare is the step when we start preparing for the big
colonization. Action is the step when we all go to Mars
and start a new civilization. We are now in the first step, so if you are interested,
you can participate in our mission to Mars. We will be appreciating this a lot.
